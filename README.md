# VaultChat

Private, decentralized and encrypted messaging built on Nostr.

No phone number.
No email.
No registration.
No central server.

Official website:
https://vaultchat.pro

---

## Features

- End-to-end encrypted messaging (NIP-44 v2)
- Decentralized communication through Nostr relays
- QR contact sharing and scanning
- Contact search
- PIN protection
- Biometric authentication
- Encrypted file transfer
- Encrypted identity backup and restore
- Multi-relay support
- No account required

---

## Why VaultChat?

Most messaging platforms require:

- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Centralized servers
- Personal information

VaultChat works differently.

Your identity is generated locally on your device and remains under your control.

No registration process.
No personal data collection.
No centralized authority.
